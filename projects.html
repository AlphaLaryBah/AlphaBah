WalletGyde

Jan 2022 to Present
What I have done:

Matchmaking Feature: I built and implemented all technical aspects of the algorithm for a matchmaking feature. Users and Financial Coaches are matched based on their responses to specific questions as they are onboarded. Users and Coaches are notified of the matching results and their next steps through the Node-mailer module. Within the Microservices Architecture contained in Docker, I worked on legacy code. Additionally, I built the PostgreSQL database where profile information and responses of Users and Coaches are stored according to data models. I constructed the logic of the matchmaking algorithm in the Express Js server and worked with both Front-end and Back-end technologies to accomplish this deliverable. I utilized the following Tech Stack:(Microservices Architecture, Docker, PostgresSQL, Sequelize, React Js, Gatsby Js, Javascript, Bootstrap, and Node-mailer).

I have built and improved many more features in WalletGyde and continue to do so.
