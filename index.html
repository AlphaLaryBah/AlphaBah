ABOUT ME

let greetings = "Hello World!";

I am Alpha Bah. I am a full-stack software developer. For the first twelve years of my life, I lived in West Africa. Codding reminds me a lot of my childhood in West Africa. It was nearly impossible to talk to one person without using at least three languages. When I walked about a few blocks away from my home, I needed to learn a new language to communicate my needs. I had to be fluent in multiple languages and dialects. Like software development, it's hard to create a fully functioning application without using numerous languages or different dialects of one.

I also come from an artistic background, Theater, drawing, painting, and music. People have asked me how it is that I enjoy software development. Considering the belief is that if a person is primarily analytical and methodical in their thinking, they are left-brained. If they tend to be more creative or artistic, they are thought to be right-brained. However, in my direct experience of software development and art, one must master both the left and right brain to be innovative and sustainable. One must be creative, practical, and not wasteful of both time and space.

I remember the first day I decided to try coding. I found a tutorial of simply the basics. To my surprise and thrill, I realized that I had been charmed away into the magnificent multiverse of software development five hours later. I was sucked in, I could not stop thinking about it, and I just wanted to keep coding.

I enjoy the practicality and creativity in molding codes. Just like a blank canvas or a piece of paper, you start with a blank screen. You add the outline, which is the wireframes and HTML, then you add the shading and the colors as CSS does for an Application. With Javascript and the back-end languages, you add functionality, and here coding goes beyond what one can do with a canvas or a piece of paper.

I appreciate and am excited about the creative parts of software development and its practical and functional role. I enjoy the fact that I can create something that people can use to improve their lives, which adds speed and consistency to our world. I enjoy learning the languages and constant innovation within them.

Thank you wolrd for stopping by.
